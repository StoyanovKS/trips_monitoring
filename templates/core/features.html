Features

Trips Monitoring focuses on the core building blocks of a practical driving logbook: vehicles, mileage, trips, refuels and reporting.

Garage

  • Add and manage vehicles.
  • Keep mileage history consistent.
  • Optional vehicle details for better overview.

Trips

  • Log trips with date, route and distance.
  • Quick overview of recent activity.
  • Helps validate mileage growth over time.

Refuels

  • Record liters, price and total cost.
  • Track fuel spending per vehicle.
  • Supports clean cost history for monthly summaries.

Reports

  • Monthly report per vehicle or for all cars.
  • Total distance, number of trips, refuels and fuel cost.
  • Quick snapshot for budgeting and analysis.

Accounts

  • User registration and login.
  • Profile page and password change.
  • Role-based behavior through groups managed by admins.
