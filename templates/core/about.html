About

This application is designed to help you monitor and better understand the costs associated with your trips or your firm trips. It allows you to track travel expenses on a regular basis, with a primary focus on fuel consumption and refueling costs. By consistently entering fuel data, you can build a clear history of your trips and spending patterns over time. The application helps you review and analyze these costs, making it easier to identify trends, inefficiencies, or unexpected changes. This ongoing overview supports smarter decisions about budgeting, route planning, and vehicle usage. Ultimately, it turns raw expense entries into meaningful insights about your travel behavior.
