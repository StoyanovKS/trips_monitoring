Application for monitoring of the costs generated by your trips

Track trips, refuels and underlying expenses.

Who may find this application useful?

Most people remember the big costs (such as insurance or tax payments), but the day-to-day expenses are what silently add up. This logbook helps you keep a clean history of your driving and fuel spending, and turns it into monthly insights.

How does it work?

  • Add your cars in one place (garage) and keep mileage consistent.
  • Log trips with distance and date.
  • Log refuels with liters, price and total cost.
  • See reports that summarize monthly totals and trends.

What you could get out of it?

  • Clear view of your monthly fuel costs
  • Better decisions about driving habits and routes
  • History you can rely on (no more “I think it was around…”)

Quick Start!

  1. Add your car
    Go to the Cars section and register your vehicle with its current mileage.
  2. Log trips and refuels
    Record each Trip and Refuel to keep mileage and fuel costs in sync.
  3. Check your reports
    Open Reports to see monthly summaries and total fuel spending.

That’s it. The more consistently you log data, the more accurate your insights become.
